Lovely Jobbly Place

Lovely Jubbly Place is type of business that offers accommodation, restaurant and bar to cater for all levels of traveler with outstanding flashpacking facilities such as a large comfortable communal area, free internet access and wifi, delicious Khmer dishes and western food, beer towers and great value cocktails in the active bar. We pride ourselves on providing immaculate service to you, our guests and friends, whether you be a basement backpacker, a fancy flashpacker, a wanderer, a businessman, a passer-through or a creature of indulgence and leisure. We employ, train and promote an outstanding cast of English-speaking Khmer characters who will make it their mission to brighten your stay and to make sure that you have a smile on your face whenever you are in our presence and we also ensure all guests are completely satisfied with their stay.
